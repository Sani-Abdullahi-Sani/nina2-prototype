# NEPL IT Procedures & Current Initiatives (Mock Excerpt for Prototype)

## Vendor / Procurement Request
To raise a procurement request: submit a Purchase Requisition via the ERP portal, obtain budget-holder
approval, then route to Procurement for vendor sourcing and PO issuance.

## New Application Onboarding
New digital applications require a Change Management Strategy, including a training plan and
a rollout communication plan, before deployment to staff.

## Current Initiatives
**Project Sigma** is an internal initiative within the IT Planning, Security & Compliance unit focused on
modernizing NEPL's application governance framework, including a review of the service catalog and
AI governance controls. It is currently in its design phase, led by the PS&C unit, with a target
pilot in Q3.

## New NEPL Organogram
The NEPL organogram was last revised to reflect the current structure under the Managing Director,
with Information Technology reporting through the Chief Technology Officer into the Managing Director's
office. A detailed org chart is maintained by HR and published on the internal SharePoint HR site.
