# NEPL HR Policy — Leave & Benefits (Mock Excerpt for Prototype)

## Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to a minimum of 21 working days of annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly.
Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance via the HR portal.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to up to 14 working days of paid sick leave per year, extendable with a medical certificate.
Annual leave continues to accrue normally during a period of sick leave; sick leave does not pause or reduce annual leave accrual.

## Study Leave
Staff pursuing approved further education may apply for study leave of up to 12 months, subject to
line manager and HR approval, and a signed return-of-service bond.

## Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave, with the option to begin up to 4 weeks before the expected delivery date.

## Disciplinary Process
Minor infractions follow a verbal warning, then a written warning, then a final written warning before
escalation to a disciplinary panel. Gross misconduct may bypass earlier stages, subject to HR review.
